places must visit

Starbucks Reserve Roastery

I had visited November 22nd, 2022

The starbucks reserve roastery is one of the biggest starbucks stores in the USA. It is filled with modern lights, technology, and amazing designs. This starbucks sells all sorts of food, even ones you may not find at your regular daily starbucks. For example, Tiramisu. I've seen the first 2 floors and they're so pretty. I took many photos and would recommend people visiting while in Chicago.



Athena Greek Restaurant

I went on December 3rd, 2022

Athena is a big, beautiful restaurant. I saw many greek mythology related statues and artwork around the store. Including nice lights and amazing food. Some orders were even made infront of you, with fire. Me and my friends enjoyed eating there and it would be a good place to come to if you like Greek Food.



Brooklyn Boulders

I went on December 3rd, 2022

Brooklyn Boulders is a bouldering, rock climbing, and even gym area. Members can go and rent shoes and harnesses for bouldering & toproping. I saw many different difficulties for bouldering and attempted many as well. The members switch out the areas every week or so to switch difficulties and get people to experience something new. Definitely a nice spot for people into being active.
